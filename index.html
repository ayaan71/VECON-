Who Are We


Value Engineering Consultancy was registered as a division of the International Advanced Safety Consultancy (IASCO) to provide Engineering Services for Buildings, Infrastructure, Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Instrument and Telecommunication projects.

Value Engineering Consultancy (VECON) was established in the year 2015 as a Project Management, Engineering and Consulting company, however due to the changing built environment and the growing needs of our Clients; it became necessary to offer multi-disciplinary Engineering Consulting Services, and Architectural Engineering under one banner.

VECON has continued to strive towards becoming a complete multi-disciplinary professionals offering our clients the specialist individual services and solutions required by any project. VECON applies teamwork and creativity on every project as the key success element. The over-all project manager is duty-bound in ensuring to have perfect control project with efficient and regular communication and control of cost, schedule and quality.

The key point of VECON is to analyse the functions of the projects and then create alternatives to achieve these functions, while maintaining the quality and the performance of all systems in the project. VECON is up to date with the latest design, standards & safety and has the necessary staff and resources to ensure that the best professional service is provided at all times.

The firm is certified by the CIVIL DEFENSE , MODON and has SCE Certified Engineers.
